My name is David and I am 37 years old. I really enjoy camping and hiking. Golf is one of those four letter words that I have a love/hate relationship with. Most of the time we get along though. Snowboarding is a passion I have had since I was a young teenager and would love to find a way to get on the mountain more. I have two sweet little girls who let me take them to Lagoon, to the park and swimming.

For the last 15 years I was working as the Component Lab Manufacturing Supervisor. I was responsible for overseeing the production of around 800 units of blood per day. The units of blood we were manufacturing were used in hospitals in Utah, Montana, Idaho and Nevada routinely and as need arose we could help out hospitals across the nation.

With my work I started doing more and more work with computers. Tracking problems, developing spreadsheets and powerpoints and some database management. This is what finally sparked the fire beneath me and got me to go back to school and work towards becoming a Database Administrator. Thus I enrolled at Salt Lake Community College and am actively pursuing this new found dream.
